About Us

Munsiyari Treks & Tours is a travel company born out of the passion and love of the locals for their hometown, Munsiyari. Nestled in the heart of Uttarakhand, our mission is to provide travelers and tourists with luxurious, comfortable, and unforgettable experiences while showcasing the pristine beauty, rich culture, and serene landscapes of this Himalayan paradise.

It is not just a travel company; it is a community-driven initiative by the people of Munsiyari who deeply understand the region's charm, heritage, and hidden gems. Our goal is to bring you closer to nature while offering the highest standards of comfort and hospitality, ensuring your visit is both rejuvenating and memorable.

Meet the Architect of Experiences


Trilok Pawar - त्रिलोक पवार

मेरा जन्म मुनस्यारी के कोटा गांव में हुआ था। 12 वीं पास के बाद पढ़ाई के साथ साथ ट्रैकिंग का काम शुरू किया, जैसे ही MA ज्योग्राफी से किया तो उसके बाद लगातार लोगों का प्यार और समर्थन रहा।

फिर ट्रैकिंग को और बड़े पैमाने पे शुरू किया 2016 से इस कार्य में एक बहुत लंबा अनुभव हो गया है जोकि उसी का परिणाम है। आज देश विदेश से लोग संपर्क करते हैं उत्तराखंड टूर-पैकेज, गाइडिंग, कैंपिंग, आदि कैलाश ॐ पर्वत, मुनस्यारी के तमाम स्थानों जैसे खालिया टॉप ट्रैकिंग-कैंपिंग और नंदा देवी ट्रैकिंग, milam ग्लेशियर, पंचाचुली ट्रैकिंग, रालम ग्लेशर, पिंडारी ग्लेशियर, नामिक ग्लेशियर, आदि



I was born in Kota village of Munsiyari. After completing my 12th grade, I started working in trekking along with my studies. As soon as I completed my MA in Geography, the continuous love and support from people followed.

Later, I began trekking on a larger scale, and since 2016, I have gained extensive experience in this field. As a result, today people from across the country and abroad contact us for Uttarakhand tour packages, guiding, camping, etc. We organize treks and camping trips to several places in Munsiyari, such as Khaliya Top Trekking-Camping, Nanda Devi Trekking, Milam Glacier, Panchachuli Trekking, Ralam Glacier, Pindari Glacier, Namik Glacier, and more.
