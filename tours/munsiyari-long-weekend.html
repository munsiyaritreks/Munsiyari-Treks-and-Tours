Munisyari Long Weekend Tour

  • Easy
  • 4D/3N
  • March-June & Sept-Nov

Munsiyari is a beautiful hill station which lies at the foothills of the Himalayan Mountains at a height of 2200 mts above sea level in the Pithoragarh district of Uttarakhand state. Munsiyar is the base for the Milam and Ralam glacier and Nanda Devi peak trek.

Munsiyari is a paradise for nature lovers and adventurous souls. Munsiyari offers you the breathtaking view of the Panchachuli peak.

Itinerary

DAY-1: Arrival at Nainital and Nainital sightseeing

Arrival at Nainital, Check-in to the hotel and take rest for a while. After some time get ready for the Nainital sightseeing. After the sightseeing tour of Nainital, way back to the hotel. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Nainital.



DAY-2: Nainital – Munsiyari

After breakfast, travel to Munsiyari. After arrival at Munsiyari, Check-in to the hotel and take some rest. You are free for exploring the nearby area or beauty of this beautiful hill station. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Munsiyari.



DAY-3: Munsiyari Sightseeing

After breakfast, get ready for the Munsiyari sightseeing tour. We take you to some famous places to see in Munsiyari like- Nanda Devi Temple, Tribal Museum, Kala Muni, etc. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Munsiyari.



DAY-4: Departure from Musiyari

After breakfast, departure from Munsiyari and our services will be finished at Munsiyari.
