Adi Kailash & Om Parvat Yatra

  • Easy
  • 8D/7N
  • March-June & Sept-Nov

Adi Kailash, one of the Panch Kailash, is the sacred abode of Lord Shiva and Maa Parvati. Along with OM Parvat, it holds deep spiritual significance in Hinduism. A mere darshan is believed to enlighten and cleanse the soul. Ravana is said to have meditated here, receiving divine blessings from Shiva.

This Yatra covers Jageshwar, Pithoragarh, Patal Bhubaneswar, Kalapani, Kuti, OM Parvat, and Adi Kailash, offering a blend of adventure and spirituality amidst temples, snow-clad peaks, serene rivers, and breathtaking landscapes.

Known as Chota Kailash, this pilgrimage allows devotees to connect with the divine presence of Lord Shiva in a serene and sacred environment.

Itinerary

DAY-1: Kathgodam – Pithoragarh (196 KMs / 7 hrs) 1627m
  • After Arriving at Kathgodam. Freshen up and have breakfast at Bhimtal.
  • Later drive to Pithoragarh. En-route visit: Neem Karoli Baba Ashram, Kanchi Dham Chittai Golu Temple Jageshwar Temple
  • Lunch en-route. Reach Pithoragarh in the Evening. Dinner and overnight Hotel.


DAY-2: Pithoragarh - Dharchula (93 Kms / 3 hrs) 940m
  • After breakfast, drive to Dharchula.
  • Reached Dharchula and we get the paperwork for the Inner line Permit.
  • After Lunch, Day free for leisure and exploring Dharchula and Nepal.
  • Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel


DAY-3: Dharchula – Gunji (71 KMs / 2.5 hrs) 3200m
  • After Breakfast, proceed to Gunji.
  • Gunji is the base village for the Adi Kailash and OM Parvat. Lunch en-route.
  • See the beautiful villages like Chialekh, Garbyang, Gunji, Napalchu
  • Reach Gunji in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at Homestay.


DAY-3: Dharchula – Gunji (71 KMs / 2.5 hrs) 3200m
  • After Breakfast, proceed to Gunji.
  • Gunji is the base village for the Adi Kailash and OM Parvat. Lunch en-route.
  • See the beautiful villages like Chialekh, Garbyang, Gunji, Napalchu
  • Reach Gunji in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at Homestay.


DAY-4: Gunji - Adi Kailash - Gunji (68 KMs) 4497m
  • Today, we will start early morning from Gunji to Adi Kailash.
  • Reach Jolingkong and head towards Adi Kailash temple.
  • Perform Pooja and Meditation at Lord Shiva & Parvati Temple at Parvati Sarovar.
  • Lunch and drive back to Gunji. En-route visit: Pandava Parvat, Pandava Fort, Parvati Mukut
  • Dinner and overnight stay at Hometay.


DAY-5: Gunji - OM Parvat - Gunji / Nabhi (22 Kms ) 4246m
  • After breakfast, leave for OM Parvat. Drive to Om Parvat. Medidate near Om Parvat.
  • En-route visit: Kali Mandir, Kala Pani, Shesh Nag Parvat, Ved Vyas Gufa
  • Return back to Gunji / Nabhi. Overnight stay and dinner at the Homestay.


DAY-6: Gunji / Nabhi - Dharchula - Didihat / Chaukori (158 KMs / 6 hrs) 3200m
  • After breakfast, drive to Dharchula.
  • Lunch en-route and later drive to Didihat / Chaukori.
  • Reach Didihat in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel.


DAY-7: Didihat - Bhimtal (189 KMs / 6.30 hrs)
  • After breakfast, proceed to Bhimtal.
  • Lunch en-route. En-route visit: Patal Bhuvaneshwar
  • Reach Bhimtal in the Evening. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel.


DAY-8: Bhimtal - Kathgodam - Delhi
  • After breakfast, proceed to Kathgodam.
  • Reach Kathgodam and transfer to Railway station/Bus stand.
  • Reach Delhi by early morning.
