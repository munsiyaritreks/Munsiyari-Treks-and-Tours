Pindari Glacier Trek

  • Moderate to Difficult
  • 8D/7N
  • Mid April-July & Sep-Nov
  • 46 KM
  • 12,000 feet

The Pindari Glacier Trek begins from Khati village, covering a total trekking distance of 46 km (round trip). This moderately challenging trek takes you through the stunning landscapes of Uttarakhand, passing through lush forests, quaint villages, and river crossings.

As you journey toward the glacier, the diverse terrain and mesmerizing views of the Himalayas make this trek an unforgettable experience for adventure enthusiasts.

Itinerary


DAY-1: Arrival in Kathgodam - Loharkhet (1,760 m)

On Day 1, arrive at Kathgodam and travel to Loharkhet (1,760m), the starting point of the Pindari Glacier trek. The 190 km journey takes 5-6 hours by private vehicle or shared taxi, passing scenic landscapes and villages. Upon arrival, settle in, rest, and acclimatize before the trek begins. Overnight stay in Loharkhet.



DAY-2: Loharkhet to Dhakuri (2680 m, 11 km trek, 6-7 hours)

On Day 2, trek 11 km from Loharkhet to Dhakuri (2,680m) in 6-7 hours. The trail ascends through forests, villages, and terraced fields, offering stunning valley views. Dhakuri’s scenic meadows provide a perfect resting spot. Settle into your campsite or guesthouse and relax. Overnight stay in Dhakuri.



DAY-3: Dhakuri to Khati (2210 m, 7 km trek, 4-5 hours)

On Day 3, trek 7 km from Dhakuri to Khati (2,210m) in 4-5 hours. The trail descends through lush forests, villages, and streams, with glimpses of snow-capped peaks and the Pindari River. Khati, the last inhabited village on the trek, offers warm hospitality and a rich cultural experience. Overnight stay in Khati.



DAY-4: Khati to Phurkia (3110 m, 16 km trek, 7-8 hours)

On Day 4, trek 14 km from Khati to Phurkia (3,240m) in 6-7 hours. The trail ascends through forests, crosses the Pinder River, and offers stunning views of Nanda Kot and Nanda Devi. Phurkia serves as the base camp for Pindari Glacier. Overnight stay in Phurkia.



DAY-5: Phurkia to Pindari Glacier (3660 m) and back to Phurkia (14 km trek, 7-8 hours)

On Day 5, trek 14 km from Phurkia to Pindari Glacier (3,660m) and back in 7-8 hours. The trail ascends through rocky terrain, offering stunning Himalayan views. Enjoy the glacier’s pristine beauty before retracing your steps to Phurkia. Overnight stay in Phurkia.



DAY-6: Phurkia to Khati (16 km trek, 7-8 hours)

On Day 6, trek 16 km from Phurkia back to Khati in 7-8 hours, descending through scenic forests and villages. Enjoy the changing landscapes, interact with locals, and unwind upon arrival. Overnight stay in Khati.



DAY-7: Khati to Dhakuri (7 km trek, 4-5 hours)

On Day 7, trek 7 km from Khati to Dhakuri in 4-5 hours, passing through scenic villages and lush forests. Enjoy the tranquility of the Himalayas before settling into your accommodation. Overnight stay in Dhakuri.



DAY-8: Dhakuri to Loharkhet (11 km trek, 6-7 hours) and drive to Kathgodam

On Day 8, trek 11 km from Dhakuri to Loharkhet in 6-7 hours, retracing scenic trails through forests and villages. From Loharkhet, drive 5-6 hours (190 km) to Kathgodam, marking the end of your Pindari Glacier adventure.
