Ralam Glacier Trek

  • Moderate to Difficult
  • 9D/8N
  • April-June & Sept-Nov
  • 80 KM
  • 12,730 feet

Ralam Glacier, near Ralam Dhura at Ralam Khal, sits at 2,290m. It is divided into Upper and Lower Ralam. The 50km trek from Munsiyari passes through Panton, Sapa Odiyar, and Lilam. Munsiyari, at the base of the Himalayan wall, serves as the trek’s starting point.

Damfu Bugyal, Ralam Glacier, and the villages of Ralam and Paton are key attractions. Kalabaland, one of the most stunning glaciers in the valley, merges with Ralam Glacier along with Sutela and Yangchar. The glacier route is about 15km from Ralam village.

The trek to Ralam Glacier is a popular adventure activity among trekkers and mountaineers. The trek usually takes around 7-8 days and involves crossing several high-altitude passes and camping in remote locations.

Itinerary


DAY-1: Kathgodam to Munsiyari (283 Km drive)

Arrive at Kathgodam railway station and take a taxi or bus to Munsiyari. The journey takes about 10-11 hours and covers a distance of approximately 280 kilometers. Overnight stay in Munsiyari.



DAY-2: Munsiyari (2290m) to Paton Village (1850m) via Lilam Village (7 Km Trek, 3 to 4 hours)

Start your day 2 from Munsiyari to Paton Village. From Munsiyari to Lilam Village you can hire taxi and then trek of 7 kiloneters to reach Paton Village. Stay Overnight in Village.



DAY-3: Paton Village to Lingurani (2300m) (11 Km Trek, 6 to 7 hours)

Start your Day 3 trek from Paton Village to Lingurani which is around 11 at distance. You will trek steep from 1850m altitude to 2300m. After reaching Lingurani, setup your tents there and stay overnight.



DAY-4: Lingurani to Killadum Camp Site (3290m) (10 Km Trek, 7 to 8 hours)

Today’s Trek will be to most challenging trek as you will gain 1000m approx. altitude from 2300m to 3290m. After reaching Killadum Camp site, setup your tents there and stay overnight.



DAY-5: Killadum to Ralam Village (3640m) (8 Km Trek, 5 to 6 hours)

Today you will trek to Ralam Village which is around 8 kilomoters from Killadum campsite. After reaching Ralam Village, setup your tents and stay overnight.



DAY-6: Ralam Village to Ralam Glacier (3900) & back to Ralam Village (9 Km Trek, 7 to 8 hours)

Today you will reach to Ralam Glacier from Village which is located at an elevation of 3900m (last reachable location). Aftere exploring Ralam Glacier and beautiful views of snow fields, glacier and peaks around, trek back to Ralam Village. Overnight stay there.



DAY-7: Ralam Village to Lungrani (18 Km Trek, 7 to 8 hours)

Trek downhill towards Lungrani from Ralam Village. Todays trek distance will be 18 km and easy as compare to others as you have to trek downhill. Overnight stay in Lungrani Campsite.



DAY-8: Lungrani to Lilam to Munsiyari via Paton Village (18km Trek, 7 to 8 hours)

Today you will trek back to Lilam Village via Paton Village and drive back to Munsiyari. Overnight stay in Munsiyari Town.



DAY-9: Munsiyari to Kathgodam (283 Km drive)

Take a taxi or bus from Munsiyari or drive back to Kathgodam with memorable memories of Ralam Glacier Trek.
